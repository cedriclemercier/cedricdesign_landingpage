What I Do

Branding

A good Brand Identity starts with a Strong Logo Design. A logo is the face of your company: It is what people think of when they hear your business name. It is the picture people have in their mind when they come across anything related to your business.

A strong logo design lays a strong foundation for consistency in every aspect of your business. Every color has a meaning, so do shapes.

A Brand Identity is consistency across various media, including:

  • Logos
  • Stationery (e.g Business Cards)
  • Advertising Banners

Video Advertising (Animated Explainer)

An animated explainer - or motion graphics video - is an excellent way to communicate a message or story effectively to an audience.

Whether it is about your service, your product, for a pitch, about the problem you want to solve, or even your company story, a short 1-min video can fit into your business strategy.

Video content is largely consumed every day and increase brand awareness. It is a much more engaging way to promote or to create content which leads to better conversions, better than long paragraphs that no one ever reads.
